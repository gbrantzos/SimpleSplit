!NOTICE!
To avoid problems with ports, reserve them
> netsh int ipv4 add excludedportrange protocol=tcp startport=4100 numberofports=200 store=persistent
> netsh int ipv4 show excludedportrange protocol=tcp
> netsh int ipv4 delete excludedportrange protocol=tcp startport=4100 numberofports=200 store=persistent
